CONTACT US

1.Google Search: You can search the name of the restaurant followed by the location (gmail"foodismrestarunt@gmail.com") on Google. The contact details usually appear in the search results.

2.Restaurant's Website: Many restaurants list their contact information, including phone numbers, email addresses, and sometimes a contact form, on their official website.

3.Social Media: Check the restaurant's social media profiles (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, etc.). Most restaurants are active on social media and may provide a way to get in touch with them there.

4.Food Delivery Apps: If the restaurant uses food delivery apps (like Uber Eats, DoorDash, or Grubhub), you can often contact the restaurant directly through the app.
